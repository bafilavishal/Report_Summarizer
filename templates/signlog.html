Frequently Asked Questions

What file types does MediEase support?

We accept PDF documents, common image formats (JPG, PNG, TIFF), and plain text files. Our system is designed for broad compatibility.

How long does it take to get a summary?

Most reports are processed and summarized within seconds to a few minutes, depending on the complexity and length of the document.

Is my data stored?

To ensure your privacy, MediEase processes your reports in real-time and does not permanently store your medical data after generating the summary.

Can MediEase replace my doctor's advice?

No, MediEase is a tool for understanding, not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for diagnoses and treatment plans.
